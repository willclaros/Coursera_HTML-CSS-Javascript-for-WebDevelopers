Chicken

Mustard chicken is a classic present in every kitchen in the world, as it requires ingredients that can be found practically anywhere. In addition to the main ingredients, to make this recipe for chicken in mustard sauce you will need heavy cream, chives (optional), salt and pepper, nothing more! We have opted for chicken fillets with mustard, but the truth is that you can prepare the same recipe with whole breasts, thighs.

Beef

Grilled meat can be a real delicacy if you know tricks to take advantage of this cooking method. We cook it without sauce but with a previous marinade that helps preserve the moisture of the meat so that when it is cut it is very juicy. They all prepare grilled meat with different methods, on metal racks over the fire. On the grill we can cook different ingredients without adding fat.

Sushi

Sushi is a typical dish of Japanese origin based on rice seasoned with rice vinegar, sugar and salt and combined with other ingredients such as raw fish, seafood, vegetables, etc. This dish is one of the most recognized in Japanese cuisine and internationally . Sushi is usually associated with fish and seafood, it can also have vegetables or eggs, or even any other accompaniment.
